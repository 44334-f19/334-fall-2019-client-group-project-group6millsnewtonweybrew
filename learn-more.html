What are Parti Yorkies?

Parti Yorkies are a breed of Yorkshire terriers that have white in their fur, rather than just blue and tan.


How do you care for Parti Yorkies?

Care for Parti Yorkies can be found on the AKC website


Is it possible for the puppies to have genetic diseases or defects?

Our puppies are bred from dogs with a genetic background clean from defects and diseases. We pride ourselves in breeding healthy puppies that are sure to make a great addition to any home.


Why is there a deposit fee and how much is it?

The deposit fee is there to ensure that the person who will be adopting one of our puppies is serious about the adoption. The deposit fee is $500 and is non-refundable.


Where are you located?

We are located in the Kansas City Metro area, but we ship nationwide.


What if there are no currently available puppies?

If there are no puppies available currently, contact us if you wish to know when a new litter will be available. Our priority is keeping our puppies and dogs happy and healthy, as such there may be a break between gestation periods.
